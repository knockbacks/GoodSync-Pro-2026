## 💡 Best Practices

* Always keep your backup destination on a separate physical drive from your source files. This protects against hardware failure.
* Run a manual sync test before you schedule a large automatic backup job.

* Use the verify option in the settings menu to ensure your copies are exact matches of your original files.
